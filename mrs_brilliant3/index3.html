Fogging

Mrs Brilliant now offers a fogging decontamination between tenancies when the property is empty, so the next tenant can have a peace of mind that the property is safe from the virus. Our Covid-19 fogging services will decontaminate your entire property and our innovative electrostatic spraying system offers long term protection (30 days) against harmful viruses and bacteria including Covid-19. it adds a protective shield on all surfaces and common touch points which kills microorganisms and stops their growth.



End of Tenancy

Mrs Brilliant offers a full end of tenancy cleaning that involves professionally clean carpets, oven, hob & extractor. Our number one promise to you is to get your deposit back in full with 100% satisfaction guaranteed service or your money back.



Neglected Properties

Our neglected property cleaning is a bespoke service specially designed for Properties that have been neglected by unruly tenants for extended period of time. The abandoned premises is often left in an uninhabitable condition with large amount of rubbish and damage left behind, then it will need specialist cleaning agents and equipment Which Mrs Brilliant team have access & knowledge to use.



Carpet Cleaning
